Tom Nook

About Tom Nook

Tom Nook is a greedy little Japanese raccoon dog. He owns a swath of businessess all under the title "Nook". Depending on your version of Animal Crossing, you can find him at a variety of places just existing for your hard-earned bells.

Pictured: Tom Nook being himself.

Fun Facts

  1. He's not just a raccoon. He's a "tanuki", a Japanese raccoon dog.
  2. Every time you pay off your loan, he swindles you into further crippling debt.
  3. He has a pair of twin nephews, Timmy and Tommy, who will soon grow up to be worse than we could ever imagine.

Friends
